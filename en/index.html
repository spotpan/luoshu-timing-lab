Method Notes | Design Axioms

Luoshu Timing Lab is not a direct copy of traditional divination, nor a deterministic prediction tool. It is an experimental symbolic framework inspired by Luoshu, yin-yang, five-element thought, and modern temporal-spatial encoding.

The rules are treated as design axioms. Their value comes from structural consistency, stable interpretation, case review, and continuous refinement.
